    • Getting Started

      The goal of sudoku is to fill in a 9x9 grid and rows with digits that are between the numbers of 1 to 9.

      At the beginning of the game, the board will have squares that are partially filled in with digits. Your job is to fill in the missing digits without having duplicate numbers in the same 9x9 grid section or row. In the image below, the number 8 is having a conflict within a vertical row. As you can see there are two 8's, which goes against the rule that you can only one of each number within a row. To fix this we need to delete the top 8 and turn it into a 9. The number 5 is having a 9x9 grid problem to fix this, we need to change the numbers that are colored in green.
